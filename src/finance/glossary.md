# Glossary

## RPO

Remaining Performance Obligations, a US disclosure metric that is used as a [forward looking measure in SaaS](https://www.thesaascfo.com/understanding-remaining-performance-obligations-in-saas/) businesses. It records the amount of contracted goods or services that have not yet been delivered and which have not been recognised and which are not cancellable.
