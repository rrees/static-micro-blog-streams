# April 2026

Julianna Barwick and Mary Lattimore's album [Tragic Magic](https://marylattimoreharpist.bandcamp.com/album/tragic-magic) contains a cover of Vangelis's *Rachel's Song* from the *Bladerunner* soundtrack. It was great live and I've enjoyed listening to it since.

I've lost track on whether [Angine de Poitrine](https://anginedepoitrine.com/accueil) are a micro-tonal cult concern on a musical sensation. I love _Fabienk_ but their live performances are just as interesting as their recordings. Certainly all their London shows are sold out for this year.

I've really enjoyed the droney sounds of the [Wind Organ](https://zimoun.bandcamp.com/album/wind-dynamic-organ-one-two) this month.
